My Favourite Food

My favourite food is Poutine. This is because poutine is a combination of some of my favourite foods: fries and cheese. Together, they create a crispy and creamy texture which boasts an enormous amount of flavour.

Furthermore, watching players like Messi dominate the field with their plays made me love the game even more.

My Favourite Show

My favourite show is Black Clover. Here are some reasons why I like Black Clover:

  1. The action scenes are creatively animated and are drawn very well, making every move enjoyable to watch.
  2. The plot is interesting and original, centering around the journey of one who was born without magic in a society where magic is everything.
  3. The writing is also high quality, and all characters act according to their natures and don't feel as if they are nothing but plot devices.
